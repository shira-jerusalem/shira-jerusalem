jerusalem

description

Jerusalem is the capital of the State of Israel and the Jerusalem District, and the largest city in Israel in terms of population size
. As of 2019, there are about 937,000 residents living there. Jerusalem is home to Israel's government institutions: the Knesset, the Supreme Court, the President's Residence, the Prime Minister's House and most government ministries
.

The city is sacred to Judaism, and in contrast, Christianity and Sunni Islam also attribute holiness to it. It was the center of the life of the Jewish people in ancient times, and the object of its longing and longing while in exile.
Because of its religious importance, the city has throughout the ages been the focus of wars and conflicts, which continue to this day.
Since the end of the 19th century, neighborhoods of the new city have developed around the Old City, which, as of the 21st century, house most of its inhabitants.
